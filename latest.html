Under the Blue Light

RAE

 

The shoot is a compilation of pictures taken under "blue light". Blue light makes some color pop and some objects reflect it. The photos taken represent my nightmares in a bizarre way. Bottom-right photo represents Mr. Grasshopper Man, he has appeared in my dreams a lot of times. Sometimes saving me but most times leading me to my doom. The photo on the bottom-left shows the mist, a mist that just twists your mind and make you see horrible things. The two at the top are the lady that led us to a place then she trapped us there. After screaming for help she blew up the place which was full of mirrors. Shards flew everywhere.
