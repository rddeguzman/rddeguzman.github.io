Who is Rae?
Rae is a transferee from Padapada National High School. He likes strange stuff, and that is why he got easily attracted to avant-garde fashion. Rae loves the color green but he uses blue things most of the time and the reason for that is unknown.
Who is Nara?
Nara is an alter personality of Rae. Nara is very experimental, bizarre, and peculiar. They love to do stuff that you wont commonly see. They are very punk rock and draws inspiration from the odd aesthetic of underground rave culture.
Why did he start sewing?
Rae decided to learn to sew so that he can feel the presence his departed grandmother. Moreover, creating something out of nothing is very satisfying for him.
